I'm a first-year undergraduate student pursuing Honours in Mathematics and Computer Science at McGill University. With a keen interest in real and complex analysis, as well as data structures and algorithms, I'm constantly looking to deepen my understanding of these fields. When I'm not studying, I enjoy pursuing my passion for photography. What started as a hobby has now turned into a side hustle, as I take up small contracts occasionally and work as a photographer for the McGill Student Association. I find immense joy in capturing moments and telling stories through my photographs. I'm always eager to learn and explore new things, and I believe that my curiosity and dedication have helped me excel both academically and creatively. Whether it's delving into mathematical proofs or experimenting with new photographic techniques, I'm always up for a challenge. I'm excited to see where my passions will take me in the future and to continue to learn and grow along the way.
